Do you want to play D&D with me?

This is an invitation to play D&D with me as your GM. I am looking for ten people for two new games starting in January, February, or March 2025.

These are paid games, charged at $16.66 USD per person per game, with three games per month minimum provided, so that is $49.98 per person per month.

Roleplay sessions usually cover four hours, with a break in between. We use Discord to communicate and Roll20 as our virtual tabletop. I can teach you how to use both systems if you are unfamiliar.


What you need

• Imagination

• Internet access

• A computer, phone, or tablet.


What you get:

• A GM who has been doing this for decades.

• Professional GM support at every level of your journey

• A new player-friendly environment

• A safe and secure place to play

• Access to my player-only Discord server

• Access to all my game compendiums to build characters with

• Pro Roll20 features (No adverts, SFX, and dynamic lighting)

• Session zero is free, plus all of your 1-on-1 backstory prep.


If you want to apply, you can email me at burrowtp@gmail.com with the following questions answered:

• Do you understand this is a paid game?

• Are you willing and able to pay for a good DND experience?

• Which timezone are you based in or would you prefer to play in?

• What is your favorite Critical Role Character?

• What idea do you have for your character backstory?


I will respond personally to each of you within 48 hours. There will be a short series of questions via email, and then if chosen you will be invited to join the Discord server.
