About

Phil Burrow

Gamesmaster • Critter • Tiger • Poet • Explorer • Writer • Adventurer

After twenty years working in energy, telecommunications, defence, TV production, and retail, I have a fantastic array of skills that I can bring to the table and use for improvisation with the Players. I enjoy games, books, and exploring the less well-traveled paths of the Lake District in search of Lanty Slee’s hidden treasure.

I have a family I am proud of and friends worldwide. I can only speak native English, but I am well-traveled and respectful of all cultures.

In 2020 I started to turn this hobby into a way of earning money. I recruited from my Youtube channel, which had a large following of roleplay fans.

In the last four years, I have successfully run Eleven Campaigns for a total of 63 People scattered across the world from various cultures, languages, and time zones. We created together and shared roleplay Adventures every week.

By June of 2024, I have run 800 sessions for my Players, in the last four years of operation.

I also run four other YouTube channels and I am a budding Historian for some of the Lake District Smuggling Legends.
